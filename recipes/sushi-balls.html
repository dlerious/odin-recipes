Spooky Sushi Balls

Description

Trick up a plate of ghoulish sushi bites, these are guaranteed to impress and give kids a healthy alternative to candy this Halloween

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups Sushi Rice
  • Smoked Salmon, to Decorate
  • Ham, to Decorate
  • Nori, to Decorate
  • Avocado, to Decorate
  • Celery, to Decorate
  • Mayonnaise, to Decorate
  • 2 Drops Orange Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Cook and cool sushi rice following packet directions, then try the ideas below.
  2. FRANKENSTEIN: Stir mashed avocado into the rice. Shape into a log. Decorate with nori, celery and mayonnaise.
  3. SCARY FACE: Shape rice into a ball. Top with smoked salmon. Decorate with nori.
  4. BLACK CAT: Shape rice into a ball. Cover with nori. Decorate with mayonnaise.
  5. JACK-O'-LANTERN: Tint rice orange with liquid food colouring. Shape into an oval. Decorate with celery and nori.
  6. MUMMY: Shape rice into a log. Decorate with nori and mayonnaise.
