Chocolate Donut Spiders

Description

Create a sweet scare this Halloween, using some deliciously simple ingredients and a little imagination!

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of Nestlé Bakers’ Choice Dark Choc Melts
  • 24 Nestlé Bakers’ Choice White Choc Bits
  • 24 Baked Pretzel Twists
  • 12 Store Bought Donuts
  • 1 Tablespoon of Vegetable Oil
  • Dark Chocolate Writing Icing

Steps

  1. Melt Nestlé Bakers’ Choice Dark Choc Melts following packet directions. Stir in 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  2. Dip one side of each donut, one at a time, in the mixture. Place on a lined tray.
  3. Use Nestlé Choc Bits and dark chocolate writing icing to make eyes. Break pretzel twists into pieces and insert into donuts to make legs. Set aside to set.
